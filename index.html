About me.

I am a Software Engineer from Baja California, Mexico. I am Software Engineer @ Nearsoft and Adjunct Professor @ the University of Baja California.

I love working while having fun at work, learning new things every day personally and technically.

I actively work to help increase diversity in the Software Industry, I participate in workshops (e.g. Django Girls),conferences (e.g. DevDay4Women) and communities (e.g. Epic Queen) to attract women to STEM Careers and particularly to the Engineering and Technology Fields.

I am interested in helping students be better prepared to land on their first real Software Engineering Job and help them acquire practice on the tools, technologies and processes that companies use (at least the ones I know about!)

Interests: Software Development, Technology, Teamwork, Travel, Reading, Education, Languages, Culture, Health, Food, Cooking.
